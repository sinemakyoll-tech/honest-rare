Interaction Design

Every animation
has a reason.

  • ✦ Custom cursor: 8px dot + 38px ring. mix-blend-mode: difference. Adapts to any background without JS detection.
  • ✦ Spring physics everywhere: [0.16, 1, 0.3, 1] bezier. Fast start, gentle land. Never linear — linear feels mechanical.
  • ✦ Scroll-triggered reveals: useInView once:true. Elements animate in as they enter the viewport — attention earned, not demanded.
  • ✦ Background videos — silent loops: No play button, no interruption. BBQ fire. Cocktail pour. Just atmosphere.
